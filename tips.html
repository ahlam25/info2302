How to Prevent and Avoid COVID-19

Here are some useful tips to help you and your loved ones avoid getting infected with COVID-19:

  • Wear a surgical mask
  • Wash habd with soap and water for at least 20 seconds
  • Avoid contact with sick people
  • Avoid touching your mouth, eyes and nose with unwashed hands
  • Do not eat raw food
  • Avoid crowded areas
  • Sleep regularly
  • Maintain physical distancing
